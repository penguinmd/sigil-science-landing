Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Introduction

Sigil Science (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services, including SMS and voice notifications sent via our automated systems.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: phone numbers and email addresses provided directly by users for the purpose of receiving notifications.
  • Usage data: message delivery status, timestamps, and interaction logs used to maintain service reliability.
  • Device information: basic technical data necessary for message delivery (e.g., carrier information).

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Delivering SMS and voice notifications you have opted into.
  • Maintaining and improving the reliability of our notification systems.
  • Responding to your inquiries or support requests.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Service providers: such as Twilio, who facilitate message delivery on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy obligations.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Message logs are retained for up to 90 days for service reliability purposes.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP to any message.
  • Request information about the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and secure storage practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
